About FURP

GUIHUA: Frontiers of Urban and Rural Planning (FURP) is an international open-access academic journal published by Springer Nature. The journal is indexed in the Emerging Sources Citation Index (ESCI) of the Web of Science Core Collection.

The journal focuses on frontier research in urban and rural planning, with particular attention to interdisciplinary topics such as urban intelligence, digital heritage, urban regeneration, planning education, land-use and transport integration, healthy cities, and sustainable development.
